The Challenge

Most outbound underperforms because the system was never built.

The gap between hiring an SDR and generating predictable pipeline is a GTM system. Without one, even strong products struggle to reach the right buyers at the right time.

1

Volume without intelligence

Reaching thousands of contacts without signal data or qualification. High activity, low conversion — and compounding damage to your domain reputation with every send.

2

Personalization without depth

AI-generated emails that reference a company name but lack real insight. Your prospects receive dozens of these daily — there's no reason for them to respond to another one.

3

Pipeline without qualification

Without a structured scoring framework, every lead looks the same. Your team invests equal effort in prospects who are ready to buy and those who never will.

The PulseGTM Framework

A 6-layer execution system.
Engineered for precision.

Our proprietary framework integrates eight proven sales methodologies into a unified outbound engine. Each layer builds on the one before it — no shortcuts, no gaps.

Layer 01

ICP & Data Foundation

Firmographic, technographic, and behavioral targeting — defining precisely who is worth reaching before any outreach begins.

Layer 02

Signal Detection & Scoring

Identifying and scoring buying signals — hiring surges, funding events, tech stack changes, leadership shifts — on a 0-100 composite scale.

Layer 03

Qualification Engine

Scoring every lead against a structured, multi-element qualification framework. Outreach only triggers when the evidence supports readiness.

Layer 04

Insight-Led Personalization

Every message opens with a relevant insight, references a verified signal, and quantifies the cost of inaction — structured, not templated.

Layer 05

Multichannel Execution

Coordinated email and LinkedIn sequences across a defined timeline. Each touchpoint follows a deliberate methodology with clear intent.

Layer 06

Feedback & Optimization

Weekly performance reviews, monthly pipeline audits, and quarterly framework recalibration — continuous improvement built into the system.
